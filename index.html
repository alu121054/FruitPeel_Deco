About FruitPeel Deco

FruitPeel Deco Info

The objective of FruitPeel Deco is to avoid contamination using natural, low cost, and efficient ingredients. We want to reduce the use of disposable things that affect the environment, flora and fauna, and also prevent more garbage, which contaminates many important places in our planet.

We made a centerpiece to fight the SDG's number 12. It is made from a mixture of citrus peels like yellow lemon, orange, pink grapefruit, and tangerine, which are known for having a good resistance, so it can be used to create firm things due to their natural structure and sturdiness.
